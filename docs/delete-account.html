CrackX Account Deletion

Last updated: April 28, 2026

CrackX users can request deletion of their account and associated app data by contacting the CrackX development team.

How to Request Account Deletion

Email fatima2bazroun@gmail.com from the email address used for your CrackX account. Use the subject line "CrackX Account Deletion Request".

Data Deleted

When an account deletion request is processed, CrackX deletes or removes access to the user's account profile, authentication record, saved simulation scenarios, CO2 assessment records, and generated report records associated with that account.

Data Retention

Some records may be retained temporarily when required for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, backups, or service operations. Backup copies, if present, are removed according to normal backup retention cycles.

Partial Data Deletion

Users may also request deletion of specific saved scenarios or generated reports without deleting their entire account by emailing the same support address.

Support

For general support, visit the CrackX Support page.
